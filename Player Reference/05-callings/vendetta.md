# VENDETTA

*They took something from you that cannot be replaced, and they are still breathing. Every prize you take is practice; every port is a place they might be. You will look them in the eye before the end. What happens after that, you have never once let yourself imagine.*

## CORE ABILITY

**NOT WHILE THEY BREATHE.** You do not get to fall — not before they do. Once per session, activate this ability to avoid suffering negative effects from Blood or Reputation fallout for the remainder of the situation.

When you create your character, answer these questions:

- Who are they, what did they take, and how long ago?
- Choose another player character. They stopped you once, an inch from revenge or ruin. Which was it?
- Choose another player character. They know the whole story. Why them?
- What do you carry or wear that your target would recognise on sight?

## MINOR BEATS

- Take Minor Blood fallout.
- Find someone who knows your target personally.
- Cost your target something: property, standing, a good lieutenant.
- Refuse an offered settlement.
- Learn something that makes your target more human.
- Put another PC in danger for the vendetta's sake.
- Meet someone else they hurt.
- Lie to an ally about where you're leading them.
- Acquire a bond who feeds you intelligence.
- Leave a message your target cannot mistake.
- Chase a lead into waters deeper than the crew agreed to sail.
- Tell another PC, in full, what was taken.
- Let hatred make the decision.
- Hear that your target knows you're coming.
- Serve someone else's grievance to feed your own.
- Spend a D8 or higher resource on a lead that dies.
- Show a mercy your target never would.
- Rehearse what you'll say when you find them.
- Learn your target has grown stronger since you started.

## MAJOR BEATS

- Take Major Blood fallout.
- Destroy a major asset of your target: flagship, stronghold, heir.
- Fall into your target's hands.
- Learn something that genuinely complicates the hate.
- Lasting harm lands on another PC or a bond because of your hunt.
- Turn down a clean way to end it without facing them.
- Find out someone hunts you the way you hunt them.
- Your target strikes first, and the crew bleeds for it.
- Ally with someone you despise against the common enemy.
- Win a real victory over them that changes nothing.

## ZENITH BEATS

- Face your target at last, and settle the account.
- With your target finally in your power, let them go.

Roll 1d10 on the following table or choose to determine what you carry with you to mark your calling:

1. A scar they gave you, never properly treated
2. Loaded pistol kept for one purpose; it has a name you tell no one
3. Their ship's name carved into driftwood
4. List of their allies and ports, kept current in your own hand
5. A letter to them you never sent, explaining everything
6. Court documents proving the wrong, that no court will hear
7. Something of theirs — taken, not given
8. A sketch of their face good enough to find them in any disguise
9. The only surviving piece of what was taken from you
10. A knotted cord counting the days since
