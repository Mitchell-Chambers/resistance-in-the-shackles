# CURSED TIDES

*When you sleep, you dream of deep water, and the dreams are getting accurate. Something beneath the waves has marked you for its own — Besmara, or a thing older than her flag. You sail because it calls you, and because staying ashore feels like drowning slowly.*

## CORE ABILITY

**SALT IN THE VEINS.** The sea keeps what it has tasted. +1 Tide protection. Once per situation, when you would mark stress to any other resistance, you may mark it to Tide instead.

When you create your character, answer these questions:

- When did the sea first speak to you, and what did it cost to listen?
- How has the call marked your body?
- Choose another player character. They watched you obey the water once. What did they see?
- Choose another player character. The sea showed you something about them. Why haven't you told them?

## MINOR BEATS

- Take Minor Tide fallout.
- Heed a warning nothing natural could have given, and be right.
- Eat or drink something of the deep.
- Let something in that a port had kept out.
- Unsettle an NPC with the way you speak about water.
- Perform a rite at a place of power, Tier 2 or deeper.
- Speak with something below the surface.
- Name the thing that calls you, and use only that name from now on.
- Wake somewhere you don't remember sailing.
- Spare a thing of the deep you could have killed.
- Meet something from your dreams while awake.
- Sink, float or lash a shrine to the water.
- Call a course on faith alone, and be right.
- Acquire a bond sworn to Besmara or the deep.
- Obey the tide against your own interest.
- Gain a mark that changes with the moon.
- Find the wakes of others who were called, and how they ended.
- Hide someone else the sea has touched.
- Stay ashore through the call's worst night, for someone else's sake.
- Refuse the call once, and feel what it takes in exchange.

## MAJOR BEATS

- Take Major Tide fallout.
- Perform a rite in a drowned ruin, Tier 3 or deeper, and receive something.
- Give the tide something you love.
- Show an outsider what calls you; watch them break, believe or run.
- A bond suffers fallout because of what follows you.
- Be recognised by something of the deep.
- Follow the call into uncharted waters (this adds D12 to the journey's resistance).
- Be claimed by Besmara's church, or the cults of the Eye, as one of theirs.
- Scuttle a safe anchorage because the tide required it.
- Meet another who was called, and answered differently.

## ZENITH BEATS

- Answer the call in full: go beneath, and do not return as anything the world knows.
- Tear the sea's claim out of yourself, and live with the silence it leaves.

Roll 1d10 on the following table or choose to determine what you carry with you to mark your calling:

1. Coral that grew around your wrist while you slept; it grows back
2. Water-ruined journal of visions; the legible pages are worse
3. Warm timber from a ship lost with all hands
4. A polite note declining your last offering — you haven't told anyone the sea writes
5. Tattoo of a creature you have met only in dreams
6. A page in your own hand, in no script you know
7. Rope that cannot be cut, in a knot you don't remember tying
8. A tooth that matches no bestiary
9. Mirror that runs half a second behind
10. A name that isn't yours, burned into the sole of your foot
