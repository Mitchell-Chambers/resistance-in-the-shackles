# HUNGER

*There is always another horizon, and behind it the thing you actually want. You tell yourself this one is the last one. The sea is wide, the take is never enough, and somewhere out there is the prize that will finally fill you up. It won't.*

## CORE ABILITY

**ONE MORE THROW.** You have never once accepted the first no. Once per session, after failing a roll in pursuit of something you want, reroll it. If you fail again, the incoming stress steps up one size.

When you create your character, answer these questions:

- What are you hunting, and what have you already spent on it?
- Choose another player character. You nearly died together going after something. What was it, and who pulled who out?
- Choose another player character. What do they own that you cannot stop noticing?
- Which rival is on the same trail, and how far ahead are you?

## MINOR BEATS

- Take Minor Luck fallout.
- Take Minor Gold fallout.
- Steal something while its owner watches.
- Follow a rumour to a Tier 2 landmark or deeper.
- Sell or sacrifice a D8 or higher resource to keep the trail warm.
- Beat a rival to a prize by hours.
- Shake on a deal you can't honour.
- Refuse pay you badly need.
- Spend your last reserve at the worst moment.
- Acquire a bond who deals in maps, secrets or stolen goods.
- Tell another PC the whole history of what you hunt.
- Walk into obvious danger because the prize might be there.
- Stand somewhere no one on the crew has stood.
- Learn that something you carry is worth more than you knew.
- Promise another PC a share to get them aboard a bad idea.
- Keep a worthless thing you cannot explain keeping.
- Give away something the moment you finally hold it.
- Win a haggle you had no business winning.
- Come back to a place you knew and find it changed beyond recognition.
- Get caught red-handed.

## MAJOR BEATS

- Take Major Luck fallout.
- Claim a prize that significant opposition guarded.
- Sell or sacrifice a D12 resource to learn what waits ahead.
- Reach a Tier 3 landmark that appears on no reliable chart.
- Betray an ally for a bigger share.
- Lose the most valuable thing you own.
- Learn the prize is cursed, guarded or worse than the stories — and keep going.
- Hold something the Aspis Consortium will kill to recover.
- Arrive at last, and find someone got there first.
- Get another PC to the thing they hunt, at real cost to you.

## ZENITH BEATS

- Claim the thing you have hunted, and pay whatever it asks.
- Reach it at last — and turn away with empty hands.

Roll 1d10 on the following table or choose to determine what you carry with you to mark your calling:

1. Brass compass that doesn't point north
2. Lockpicks worn smooth with use
3. Chart of a place no official map shows, annotated in three hands
4. Coin from a mint that no longer exists
5. Letter of provenance for an item you don't own yet
6. List of everyone who hunted it before you, with notes on how they ended
7. Treasure map you drew yourself as bait, and have started to believe
8. Spyglass with a cracked lens you refuse to replace
9. Wax-sealed clay jar, contents unknown
10. Three maps of the same coastline that don't agree
