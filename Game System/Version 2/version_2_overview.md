## Protection Matrix — Verified

Each resistance appears as a named +2 in exactly **four** classes and is absent from exactly **two**:

| Resistance | +2 Named Pair In | Absent From (thematic read) |
|---|---|---|
| **Blood** | Castaway, Corsair, Daredevil, Errant | Sharper, Sin Bosun *(soft hands; the body is not the instrument)* |
| **Tide** | Castaway, Tidecaller, Sin Bosun, Chronicler | Sawbone, Errant *(the two rationalists — no defence against what can't be diagnosed)* |
| **Reputation** | Tidecaller, Corsair, Sharper, Sawbone | Powder Mage, Daredevil *(the legend is wild and unmanaged; "sanity, safety, reputation — all secondary")* |
| **Luck** | Powder Mage, Sharper, Daredevil, Sin Bosun | Castaway, Chronicler *(luck already abandoned one; knowledge is no defence against fate for the other)* |
| **Gold** | Powder Mage, Sawbone, Chronicler, Errant | Tidecaller, Corsair *(the sea takes its tithe; every coin spent in port)* |

No same two +2s shared by any two classes. Every class's generic +1 picklist excludes its missing resistance.

## Coverage Notes

**Core skills:** 9 of 11 skills appear as a core (Hunt twice: Powder Mage, Errant). Never core: **Carouse, Plunder** — both are everywhere as named/picklist grants; acceptable, but a future class could anchor on either.

**Core domains:** 8 of 9 appear as a core (Arcane twice: Tidecaller, Powder Mage; Deepwater twice: Corsair, Sawbone). Never core: **Shallows** — present in every universal picklist instead.

**Forbidden skills:** All 11 skills are forbidden somewhere; no two classes share the same forbidden pair. Most-forbidden: Command and Endure (3 each). Unique distinctions: only the Sawbone is denied Hunt; only the Corsair is denied Reckon; only the Errant is denied Navigate; only the Daredevil is denied Tend.

**Heart donor coverage:** All nine Heart base classes are used at least once. Doubled donors (Witch, Hound, Incarnadine, Junk Mage, Vermissian Knight) never lend the same ability twice — e.g. the Witch's Crucible went to the Sharper while her True Form/Familiar/spell-suite went to the Tidecaller.

**Universal picklist domains:** Deepwater, Shallows, Outlaw appear in all ten domain picklists (per the agreed universal trio).

**Roll formula:** Every active major rolls [Skill] + the class's core domain, with the Chronicler's Ancestor Rites and similar staying on-domain.

**Weapon notation:** Heart's `Kill DX` is replaced by tagging each weapon with the attack skill that suits its natural use — **Brave** (desperate, personal melee: cutlasses, knives, mauls, swung lanterns), **Hunt** (deliberate or ranged violence: pistols, duelling swords, harpoons, hexes), **Plunder** (breach-and-board tools: boarding axes, excavators' picks). The tag marks the weapon's natural grip; the GM may allow another applicable skill at no bonus, as with any untagged equipment. Class equipment never leans on a skill its class forbids (e.g. the Sawbone, forbidden Hunt, carries no ranged weapons; the Sharper, forbidden Brave, fights only with Hunt-tagged tools — the exception is the Chronicler's lantern-staff, a deliberately desperate Brave D6 in untrained hands).

## Outstanding System Dependencies

1. **Location tiers (0–4)** — drafted inline in the Castaway (Islandsblood); needs its own system document. Also referenced by the Wild Hunt and the Sharpers'/Sawbone's tier-adjacent language.
2. **Hull stress track** — assumed from Pass 1 by Sin Bosun (Storm Calling), Corsair (Boarding Action), Powder Mage (Powder Keg), Sawbone (Living Ship, equipment).
3. **Wyrd tag** — kept in Castaway's WHAT CAME BACK pending a Shackles rename (*Fathomless*? *Tide-touched*?).
4. **Journey rules (Wildsea)** — referenced by Wild Hunt, Trailblazer-descendants, Scrying Bowl, Living Ship; terminology assumed as "journey/voyage."
5. **Named-deity guidance** — Pass 1's Divine Sanction (Sin Bosun) was cut from cores; decide whether it lives in character creation or a calling.

## Balance Watch-List (flagged in individual design notes)

- Sharper's DOUBLE OR NOTHING — deliberate breach of "Luck only clears when fallout fires"; needs sign-off.
- Sawbone's THE IMPOSSIBLE SURGERY — strongest sub-minor in the set; demote to Minor-fallout-only if Blood trivialises.
- Powder Mage's SHATTERING LOAD — protection-to-0; soften to −2 if it dominates.
- Castaway/Chronicler/Powder Mage casting-mastery engines — self-balancing like their Heart originals, but Blood fallout (Powder Mage) punishes harder than Mind did.
- Errant's READ THE FIGHTER and Daredevil's MISDIRECTION — uncosted; gate per-situation if overused.
- Tidecaller's Tide economy — pact loop + Prior Claim may make Tide too comfortable for the one class meant to handle it.
