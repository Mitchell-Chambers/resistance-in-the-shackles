# Chronicler — Draft: Combat-to-Par + Knowledge-Spike Trim

*Implements the re-grade action for the Chronicler: **Combat 1 → par (3)** and **Knowledge +9 → ~+5** (the biggest single driver of the set's Knowledge over-shift). The elegant part: the budget the class must now spend reaching the Combat floor comes **straight out of** the over-built Knowledge pile — the fix and the trim are one move.*

**Identity preserved.** The Chronicler stays the drowned-lore scholar — Knowledge remains its single signed-off Proficiency. It does not become a fighter; it becomes a scholar **whose recovered dead-empire lore is itself a weapon.** Combat runs on **Plunder** (the excavator's pick) and **recovered war-magic cast on Reckon+Ruins** — never Brave or Command (both forbidden, and neither is the fantasy).

**The budget move in one line:** two redundant "ask-the-GM" info minors are merged/folded, and one knowledge major is converted to a relic-weapon; the freed slots become the combat content. Net: Knowledge down ~5, Combat up ~2, total roughly flat.

---

## NEW — Combat content (brings Combat 1 → 3)

### Minor — THE EXCAVATOR'S ANSWER  *(Plunder)*
*You have spent a career learning where old stone wants to break, where a sealed tomb's hinge is weakest, what a millennium does to bone. The same eye reads the things that guard those places.*

Gain the **Plunder** skill. Your excavator's pick (and like tools) is a weapon in your hands. Against **stone, bone, construct or undead** — the things that haunt ruins and wrecks — your attacks gain the **Piercing** tag; you know exactly where the centuries left them weak.

### Major — THE LIGHT OF DROWNED AZLANT  *(now a relic-weapon; converted from pure utility)*
*The first empire made light do things light does not do — and not all of it was for seeing by. The sea took them anyway, but it kept their lamps, and some of those lamps were built for war.*

Roll **Reckon+Ruins** to call the cold, searing light of Azlant. It functions as a weapon: **(Hunt D8, Ranged)** — a lance of impossible brightness that burns where it lands.

- **MINOR: THE REVEALING BEAM.** Instead of burning, spread the light across an area until the end of the situation: it illuminates as full daylight, and the invisible, hidden and disguised are shown as what they are. *(This is the ability's original utility function, retained as an option.)*
- **MINOR: LIGHT BELOW.** The light works perfectly underwater and can be sent ahead — down a passage, into a wreck, through a hatch — for you to see what it touches.
- **MINOR: THE HONEST HOUR.** While the light holds, lies discolour the air as they are spoken. You see the stain; so does everyone else.

*(Plus existing **THE CYCLOPS CANTO** — already combat — gives the third unit. Combat now ≈ par.)*

## TRIMMED — the Knowledge spike (+9 → ~+5)

### Merged → THE DIG MAPPED  *(replaces Excavator's Eye + The Annotated Chart)*
*One eye on the chart of what drowned, one on what's worth pulling out of it.*

Gain the **Navigate** skill. Once per session, when seeking a wreck, ruin or drowned settlement, ask the GM where to start looking, and they will tell you. When you reach a site, ask what the single most valuable thing you could extract is — and what extracting it will cost. *(Two overlapping "ask-the-GM" minors collapsed into one; removes a redundant info-tap that the re-grade flagged as NOSE-grade information better owned by the Sharper.)*

### Folded into the core — OBJECT READING
*Object-reading is the archivist's defining act; it belongs in the thesis, not as a separate tap.*

**THE ARCHIVE** (core) gains: *once per session, when you handle a significant object with a minute's attention, you learn who made it, when, what it was used for, and the most significant event it witnessed.* The standalone OBJECT READING minor is removed; its slot is freed.

### Reclassified — TONGUE OF THE DEAD becomes colour
Reading languages you already know is, per its own design note, "not a stake." It stays on the sheet as **flavour** (you can read scripts you've encountered; new ones are a Reckon+Ruins roll) but no longer counts as a scored Knowledge ability.

### Untouched knowledge identity (the Proficiency that stays)
**THE ARCHIVE** (core, now with object-reading), **ACADEMIC NETWORK**, **THE OLD WAY**, and the divination/lore majors **THE SAOC STARMAPS**, **THE ANCESTOR RITES**, **RUIN SOVEREIGN**, plus the flex **SPELL BEYOND MEMORY**. This is still a deep, distinct Knowledge proficiency — just no longer a runaway spike.

## Extreme sign-off (required)

The Chronicler remains a deliberate Extreme and needs explicit approval, now reading:

- **One Proficiency: Knowledge (+5).** A signed-off moderate spike — the scholar's defining strength. Approved by design.
- **Combat at par (floor met).** No longer below the floor. ✓
- **Neglect: Heal (0).** A scholar who doesn't heal. No Tend Blood kit, so this is intrinsic, not fixable by redistribution — and fine as identity (like the Castaway's wordlessness). Approve as intentional.
- **Social near par (1).** Effectively at the low edge of par, not a hard neglect — acceptable.

So the Chronicler ends as a **one-Proficiency, one-Neglect** class plus the universal forbidden-resistance — exactly the default shape the median allows, with Combat lifted to the protected floor. The runaway-spike problem is resolved.

## Balance pass (against the tag glossary)

- **THE EXCAVATOR'S ANSWER — Piercing, conditional.** The pick is already *Plunder D8, Brutal, Tiring* in the class's equipment, so granting its use isn't new power. Piercing is gated to stone/bone/construct/undead only — narrower than Heart's *Unmaking Claws* (unconditional Piercing on a minor), so **more conservative** than precedent. Safe.
- **THE LIGHT OF DROWNED AZLANT — (Hunt D8, Ranged).** Compared to the class's other relic-weapon major, *The Cyclops Canto* (Hunt D6, Spread, Debilitating, One-Shot): the Light is single-target D8 with no rider tags and no One-Shot, the Canto is D6 AoE with two riders. Comparable major-tier power, different shape. Cast on Reckon+Ruins like the rest of the suite. Safe.
- **Gates** are session/situation only — no D3s, conforming.
- **Flag for the class file:** confirm THE EXCAVATOR'S ANSWER's Piercing and THE CYCLOPS CANTO don't both apply to the same strike (different actions; shouldn't combo).

*Ability text is a working draft in house style; counts are ±1 estimates — re-tally after the rewrite to confirm Combat lands at floor and Knowledge sits in the +4/+5 band.*
