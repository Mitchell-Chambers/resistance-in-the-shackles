# Heal Redistribution — Draft (Castaway · Corsair · Tidecaller)

*Implements the back half of the Sawbone shift. With the Sawbone turned ship-ward, everyday **party-Blood** healing moves to the three moderates that already carry a Tend Blood kit. Each gains one ally-heal, funded zero-sum by a one-point trim of its over-axis — trims that also ease the set's Social/Knowledge over-shift a little further.*

**Why these three:** Castaway, Corsair and Tidecaller are the Tend-Blood-kit classes sitting at/just-above par on Heal, with their healing currently aimed mostly at themselves. The other Tend-kit classes are already covered (Sin Bosun keeps Ministrations + Intercession; Errant keeps Protector's Stance). The non-Tend classes (Sharper, Daredevil, Chronicler) stay at Heal 0 by design — intrinsic neglect, not the lever.

---

## Castaway — ally-reach for island medicine

### Upgraded — CRAB AND ROOT  *(Tend)*
*You learned medicine from an island pharmacopoeia no physician has catalogued — and you can put a crewmate back together with it as readily as yourself.*

Gain the **Tend** skill. You can treat Blood stress and fallout — your own **or an ally's** — without equipment or supplies, using whatever grows, crawls or seeps nearby. When you treat an **ally** this way, your Tend dice increases by **one step**.

*Funding: trim one Skill minor (Castaway runs +2 over par on Skill). **SPEAKING TO STRAYS** is the cut — its once-per-session animal distraction is colour-grade and overlaps the Wild-Hunt fantasy; its kin-with-the-wild flavour survives in the class's fiction. Net: Skill −1, Heal +1.*

---

## Corsair — the rough ship's surgeon

### New minor — BELOW THE WATERLINE  *(Tend)*
*You have closed your own wounds in a rolling sea by lamplight; you have closed worse in your crew. It is not gentle work. It holds.*

Gain the **Tend** skill. Once per situation, you patch a nearby ally in the middle of things — remove **D6 Blood stress** from them; or, with your sea-chest surgeon's kit to hand, downgrade one **Minor Blood fallout** instead. The crew has learned not to look.

*Funding: trim one Social minor (Corsair runs +2 over par on Social, a dual-Proficiency it keeps). **HARBOUR LEGEND** is the cut — its "someone stands you a drink in every port" is the softest of the social perks and overlaps EARNED FEAR / VIOLENCE AS ARGUMENT. Net: Social −1, Heal +1. (Corsair's existing OLD SALT'S STITCHES keeps its self-treatment role.)*

---

## Tidecaller — sea-medicine for the crew

### Upgraded — WITCH'S HANDS  *(Tend)*
*The sea is a pharmacopoeia for those it has claimed, and you have learned to share what it gives you.*

Gain the **Tend** skill. When you have time to prepare, you turn a resource with the Deepwater or Wilderness domain into a draught; when drunk, it removes Blood or Tide stress equal to its dice size minus one step — choose which when you brew it. In addition, once per situation you may treat a nearby ally directly with sea-physick, removing **D4 Blood stress** without a prepared draught.

*Funding: trim one Knowledge minor (Tidecaller runs +4 over par — a mild spike). **DEEPSPEAK** is the cut — it is one of several "ask a sea-creature / read an omen" taps (THE DROWNED PLACES, HEX SIGHT, FAMILIAR, THE SCRYING BOWL all remain); its kinship-with-Besmara's-animals flavour survives in DEEPSPEAK's sibling abilities. Net: Knowledge −1, Heal +1.*

## Balance pass (against the tag glossary & Heart precedents)

- **CRAB AND ROOT — +1 step treating allies.** Dice-step on conditional Tend is the Sawbone's own BONESETTER grammar ("Tend dice increases by one step"). Minor-tier, safe.
- **BELOW THE WATERLINE — D6 ally Blood, once/situation.** Lighter and more gated than its Heart ancestors: the Vermissian's GET BEHIND ME (major) removes D4 *and* gives standing +1 ally protection; the Incarnadine's BROKER (major) removes D6 from any track. A once-per-situation D6 Blood-only heal is correct for a **minor**. Safe.
- **WITCH'S HANDS — D4 direct ally heal added.** The draught already healed allies; the addition is a small (D4, once/situation) un-prepared option. Below major-tier ally-heals. Safe.
- **Gates** session/situation only — no D3s.
- All three run on **Tend**, which every recipient already has in its kit and (for Corsair) gains cleanly.
