# Version 4 — Overview

Version 4 is the aggressive rebuild: each class cut to its essentials and its perfect-balance fits (which favours direct Heart ports), with the gaps refilled from **unused Heart material** rather than invented mechanics. V3's per-session band-aids are gone — anything that needed a frequency gate to be acceptable was either rebuilt with a real cost/roll or replaced outright.

Structure is unchanged throughout: 2 cores, 13 minors (3 generic + 10 named, 2 bare +2), 5–6 majors × 3 subs, 3 zeniths; protection matrix, forbidden skills, core combos, and all 30 zeniths carry forward untouched.

---

## The Three Moves

**1. Cut, don't gate.** Abilities that were fiat at any frequency were removed: The Perfect Lie, The Killing Word, Leverage, Dread Name, The Point That Ends Arguments, Weapon of Choice, Cold Read, Dead Drop Network, Read the Omen, Triage.

**2. Refill from Heart's unused shelf.** Every donor class still had unported abilities; V4 spends them:

**3. Wire everything into the dice.** Remaining originals roll, cost stress, or answer one rationed question. The Daredevil's escapes now all feed the Luck track or roll with fail-forward; the Errant's duelling engine is voluntary risk-for-damage (the doom engine's own grammar); the Sharper's two cores both ride Luck from opposite directions.

## Verification Targets

- Certainty language (player-facing): V2 = 49, V3 = 32, **V4 target ≈ 20**, all colour-grade keeps (sensory perks, ask-grammar "they will tell you", Mark of Shadow "never Risky" grammar — each has a printed Heart precedent).
- Gates: session / situation / journey / campaign only. No D3s.
- Sphere scatter: every class now has at least one ability outside its home sphere (Sharper gained combat; Sin Bosun gained a social weapon it already had; Daredevil's info major now rolls).
