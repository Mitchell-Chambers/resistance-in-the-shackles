# Powder Mage

_Magic and gunpowder have one thing in common: they both kill you if you handle them carelessly. You have learned to handle both carefully enough that it is everyone else who dies. You channel arcane force through iron and smoke, stitching spellwork into the moment of firing, turning a pistol shot into something that defies physics and good sense. In the colonial navies they would have burned you for it; in the Shackles they just want to know what it costs to hire you. The answer is more than they think, because the real price is not paid in gold. The magic is eating through you — slowly, inexorably, a grain at a time, the way a slow match eats cord. You have decided the price is worth it. The power is undeniable; addictive, in fact. To hold the moment of detonation in your mind, to feel every charge and loaded barrel within thirty paces like sparks behind your eyes — there is nothing else like it, and safety, solvency and your own flesh have all become secondary to the next impossible shot._

## Core Traits
- SKILL: Hunt
- DOMAIN: Arcane
- RESOURCE: A horn of powder cut with ground witch-salts D6 Arcane
- EQUIPMENT: Pick one:
    - Two old-fashioned pistols Hunt D8, Ranged, Loud, One-Shot
    - Witch-etched duelling pistol Hunt D6, Ranged, Brutal, Dangerous
    - Overstuffed coat of tools and charms and a boot-pistol Tend Blood D6; Hunt D6, Ranged, Reload

## CORE ABILITIES

### POWDER BOND
_The tingle in your fingers tells you where the fire is._ You can detonate any unattended powder charge or loaded firearm within thirty paces by will alone; powder carried or worn by a person is shielded by its bearer's luck — detonating it is an attack, rolled Hunt+Arcane. You always know the location of gunpowder, loaded weapons and other ignition sources nearby — including magical equivalents: fire-workings, alchemical bombs, anything waiting to burn.

### BURN THE BODY
_The magic is eating through you, and you have learned to lean into the heat._ When your Blood stress is 4 or higher, roll with mastery when you attempt to cast a spell.

## MINOR ABILITIES

### BEEN EVERYWHERE
Gain access to one of the following domains: Deepwater, Shallows, Outlaw, Arcane, Colonial, Faith, Ruins. You can take this advance more than once.

### BY ANY MEANS
Gain access to one of the following skills: Brave, Carouse, Deceive, Hunt, Navigate, Plunder, Reckon, Tend, Vanish. You can take this advance more than once.

### CALLED SHOT
_Wind, distance, angle, the target's movement — the answer arrives complete._ Gain the Reckon skill. When you name a specific point before firing — a hand, a rope, a lock, a lantern — and succeed, the shot hits exactly that point. This is not magic. It is calculation.

### DEEP POCKETS
_Your coat is overstuffed with the trade's leavings: doctored powder, fuse-cord, charms against damp, and three things you could sell if it came to that._ Gain +2 Gold protection.

### FALSE THUNDER
_Besmara's thunder and yours are indistinguishable from the deck of a sinking ship._ Gain the Faith domain. When you use SLOW MATCH, you may also consume resources with the Faith domain.

### GHOL-GAN FIRE
_You have recovered pre-human incendiary formulae from drowned stonework, and improved them._ Gain the Ruins domain. Choose one alchemical round you know how to brew: incendiary (the target catches fire, marking D4 stress each round until extinguished), concussive (the target's next action is Risky) or smoking (the area gains the Obscuring quality). You can carry three at a time; restocking takes an hour of downtime.

### NAVY GUNNER
_You learned the great guns on a colonial deck before the magic found you._ Gain the Colonial domain. You can crew, aim and command cannon of any navy's pattern, and you know their drill, their signals and the layout of their magazines from the inside.

### QUICK DRAW
_They didn't catch you without a weapon. They simply didn't find the one you had._ Gain the Brave skill. Regardless of searches, disarmament or social convention, you always have something small, loaded and sufficient.

### SIPHON OF FORTUNE
_There are tables in Port Peril where you can stake more than coin; your wager of choice is good luck._ Gain +2 Luck protection.

### SLOW MATCH
_Powder burned with intention is a prayer the universe has to answer._ Gain the Tend skill. Before you cast a spell from this class, you can opt to destroy a resource with the Arcane domain. Roll the resource's dice; the amount rolled is added to your Protection value against any stress incurred as a result of casting the spell.

### SMOKE AND MIRRORS
_The smoke goes where you tell it._ Gain the Vanish skill. When you fire, you can direct the smoke to obscure vision, and you may roll Vanish with mastery to reposition unseen behind it.

### THE POWDER TRADE
_Your appetite put you deep into the networks that move powder through the Shackles._ Gain the Outlaw domain. You always know where to source powder in any port regardless of legality, and the smugglers who move it consider you a customer worth keeping alive.

### WARDING SPELLS
Gain +1 Protection in the Blood, Tide, Luck or Gold resistance. You can take this advance more than once.

## MAJOR ABILITIES

### ARCANE LOAD
_You stitch the working into the charge itself, and the shot carries it._ Roll Hunt+Arcane to lay a working into a loaded weapon; this takes a few minutes, and a weapon holds one working at a time. When the weapon is fired, the shot is Seeking: it bends toward its target, ignoring cover and intervening obstacles.
- MINOR: SHATTERING LOAD. When you lay a working, you may choose this instead: on a hit, the target's Blood protection is reduced by 2 until the end of the situation.
- MINOR: OVERCHARGE. When you lay a working, you may choose this instead: the shot's stress dice increases by two steps, and you mark D4 Blood stress when it is fired.
- MINOR: TWO BARRELS. You may have two laden weapons at once, and you may lay workings into an ally's weapon as easily as your own.

### POWDER KEG
_Everything within thirty paces is a bomb if you are standing near it._ Once per session, you may detonate a powder magazine, a cannon battery or a large alchemical store. The explosion inflicts D10 Blood stress on everyone in the area and Hull stress on any vessel present, destroys the source entirely, and renders the area uninhabitable for the rest of the situation. You mark D6 Blood stress from the expenditure.
- MINOR: CONTROLLED BURN. Designate one person or object in the blast radius that takes no stress from the detonation. Everything else still burns.
- MINOR: DISTANCE DETONATION. You can trigger POWDER KEG from anywhere, provided you have sensed the powder with POWDER BOND. You do not need to be nearby. You do not need to watch.
- MINOR: CHAIN DETONATION. Every powder charge and loaded weapon within the blast radius detonates as well, potentially expanding the zone. The GM determines the cascade.

### SPARK IN THE CHAMBER
_You snap your fingers and the air inside them remembers it wanted to be fire._ This spell functions as the following weapon: (Hunt D6, Piercing.) If the target is carrying powder or a loaded firearm, it inflicts D8 stress.
- MINOR: THE SLOW BURN. Targets you harm with SPARK IN THE CHAMBER catch fire, marking D4 stress each round until extinguished.
- MINOR: SMOKELESS. The spark leaves no flash, no smoke and no evident cause. Unless someone knows what a powder mage is and that one is present, the death reads as a terrible accident.
- MINOR: COIN-GOLD BLOOD. You gain Protection 5 against stress marked due to flame or heat.

### VEIL OF SMOKE
_A battlefield is a text written in smoke, and you hold the pen._ Roll Vanish+Arcane to cast this spell. On a success, powder smoke floods an area roughly the size of a ship's deck and obeys you: all Vanish checks within it are made with mastery, and ranged attacks into or through it are Risky. The veil lasts until the end of the situation, or until you leave it.
- MINOR: CHOKING CLOUD. Adversaries inside the veil who need to breathe treat every action as Risky until they leave it.
- MINOR: SHAPES IN THE SMOKE. The smoke shows your enemies things that are not there. While the veil holds, the GM treats your allies' numbers and positions as unknown to adversaries, even ones who watched them enter.
- MINOR: THE SIGNAL FIRE. A fire you kindle can be seen clearly for twenty miles, in any weather. What it says, and to whom, is up to you.

### THE DUELLIST'S ART
_One opponent, agreed terms, and mathematics._ In single combat with a named opponent, your shots gain the Piercing tag and you never suffer increased difficulty from environmental conditions. You were built for this.
- MINOR: PERFECT SHOT. Once per situation, after at least one exchange, declare a shot perfect: roll with mastery and increase your stress dice by two steps.
- MINOR: READ THE DRAW. You always know when your duelling opponent is about to fire. Once per situation, you may interrupt their action with a shot of your own, rolling as normal, before their attack resolves.
- MINOR: SECOND GUN. In a duel, you always have a loaded weapon ready, as though you had prepared for this specific opponent. The first exchange cannot catch you without options.

### THE HIDDEN MAGAZINE
_Every coast in the Shackles holds a dry cache someone died before emptying. You can smell them._ Roll Reckon+Arcane to cast this spell. On a success, you find (or spontaneously discover) a path to a hidden powder store nearby — a smuggler's cache, a wrecked warship's intact hold, a battery abandoned with its magazine sealed. When you work there, remove D6 stress from Blood; your allies may use it the same way. The path seals after an hour or so — with warning, so you'll have time to leave.
- MINOR: BENEVOLENT. You or any ally may remove D6 stress from Luck or Gold instead.
- MINOR: OMNIPRESENT. You access the same magazine every time, no matter where you are. At the GM's discretion, Major Luck fallout could see it found and emptied by someone else.
- MINOR: BOUNTIFUL. Once per session, when you access the magazine, you can locate a non-unique item of D8 value or lower.

## ZENITH ABILITIES

### THE FINAL CHARGE
_Every grain of arcane force your body has stored, released at once._ Once, you can let it all go. The detonation is apocalyptic at scale — a fortress, a fleet, a harbour. Everything within a wide radius takes maximum stress; what the GM rules destroyed is destroyed. You survive, barely, but your body will never hold arcane force again: every ability of this class burns out of you with the blast. You live. You are no longer a powder mage. Whether it was worth it is the question your arc was always asking.

### THE PERFECT SHOT
_The universe finally agrees that you are right._ Once, at a moment of your choosing, you make a shot of impossible precision — through a fortress wall, across an ocean, backward into an old wound that never closed. It lands, and the target suffers its full consequence. There are no conditions and no defence. But the universe's agreement is purchased, not given, and the price is the one you have been paying all along, compounded: the recoil takes everything that is left of you. The shot is the last thing you do, and the best.

### THE UNDYING SPARK
_You stop carrying the fire and become it._ At the heart of a great burning — a magazine, a fireship, a battery in full roar — you let the boundary go. Your body is unmade, and what you were disperses into every charge of powder in the Shackles. From now on, when desperate gunners pray over a wet charge that fires anyway, when a pistol misfires in the hand of a murderer at exactly the right moment, when a powder mage yet unborn feels the first tingle behind their eyes — that is you, or what you have become. The GM determines when and how the spark shows itself. It never speaks. It always burns.

## Design Notes (not player-facing)

**Sources & rebuild rationale:** This is the reckless-addict slice of the Junk Mage: BURN THE BODY is Ravening Knowledge with Blood standing in for Mind (the cost is the body, not the sanity), and SLOW MATCH is the SACRIFICE core demoted to a minor, with FALSE THUNDER as its extension exactly mirroring Litanies of False Power. THE HIDDEN MAGAZINE is Sanctum of the Stone Chorus rebuilt; SPARK IN THE CHAMBER is Kiss of the Drowned Queen (powder-carrier replaces shin-deep water as the D8 condition, which at sea fires about as often); COIN-GOLD BLOOD is borrowed verbatim from Fire of the Red King; the equipment block's twin pistols are the Junk Mage's own.

**Aggressive cuts from Pass 1:** Arcane Load is now a single major with the three effects split across base + sub-minors (it was an overloaded core). Powder Bond loses the fifty-pace numbers game and keeps the fantasy. Cut entirely: Detonation Field (redundant with Powder Keg), Rapid Reload, Distant Death, Guided Bolt, Powder Mage's Sight, Fire Blindness, Two Pistols, Overclock, Iron Constitution, Arcane Amplifier, The Cost Made Flesh, Fire and Smoke Command (folded into Veil of Smoke/Powder Keg). Most were unpriced passive power Heart wouldn't allow at minor tier; the best fragments survive as sub-minors or knack candidates (Pistoleer already covers some of this in the skills doc).

**Checklist compliance:**
- Core combo Hunt+Arcane — unique. Cores: identity (Powder Bond) + engine (Burn the Body).
- Minors: 3 generic + 10 named (4 skill+text: Reckon, Vanish, Tend, Brave; 4 domain+text: Outlaw, Colonial, Faith, Ruins; 2 bare +2). Named skills incl. core: 5. Named domains incl. core: 5 — matches the Junk Mage exactly.
- Protection matrix: **Gold 2 (Deep Pockets), Luck 2 (Siphon of Fortune), no Reputation** — excluded from WARDING SPELLS and granted nowhere. Thematically straight from the Junk Mage: *sanity, safety, reputation; all these are secondary.*
- **Forbidden skills: Endure, Command** — the glass cannon who burns rather than endures, and follows no one's drill but their own.
- Majors: 6 (spell-heavy, matching the Junk Mage) — weapon suite (Arcane Load), area (Powder Keg), weapon (Spark), area/control (Veil), fighting technique (Duellist's Art), spatial/support (Hidden Magazine). No category exceeds two.
- Zeniths: 3 — survival at total cost, sacrificial perfection, Herald-style dissolution into the element.

**Open questions:**
1. SHATTERING LOAD (protection to 0) is the strongest sub-minor here; if it dominates play, soften to "reduced by 2."
2. BURN THE BODY rewards sitting at high Blood stress, which the fallout system punishes naturally — same self-balancing loop as Ravening Knowledge, but worth watching since Blood fallout is more punishing than Mind.

**Version 3 changes:** POWDER BOND no longer detonates carried powder without an attack roll; GHOL-GAN FIRE knows one round type; SMOKE AND MIRRORS repositioning is a mastery Vanish roll; SHATTERING LOAD reduces protection by 2, not to 0; PERFECT SHOT converted to mastery + two dice steps; duel and scene gates standardized to situation.
