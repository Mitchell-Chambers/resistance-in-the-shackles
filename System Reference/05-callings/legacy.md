# LEGACY

*Your name went to sea before you did. Somebody of your blood did something these islands still remember, and every port has already decided who you are. The legacy is a door, a debt and a loaded gun. The one thing it has never been is yours.*

## CORE ABILITY

**IN THEIR FOOTSTEPS.** You have watched this done — in stories, in dreams, in blood. Gain one skill your ancestor was known for. Once per session, before you roll dice to resolve an action, instead state that your result is a 6. You succeed but take stress.

When you create your character, answer these questions:

- Who came before you, and what did they do that the sea still remembers?
- What do strangers expect of you on hearing your name?
- Choose another player character. Your families' stories are tangled — debt, rescue or crime. Which, and who knows?
- Choose another player character. They treat you differently since learning the name. How?

## MINOR BEATS

- Take Minor Reputation fallout.
- Be recognised for the name before the face.
- Hear something about your ancestor you'd rather not know.
- Catch yourself making their famous decision.
- Refuse a piece of the inheritance.
- Take up a piece of it you once refused.
- Make amends to someone your blood wronged.
- Be turned away for something done before your birth.
- Recover a record, a chart or a D8 or higher resource of your line.
- Learn the famous deed was a lie.
- Gain a follower because of who you come from.
- Do what your line would never do, and say so aloud.
- Meet the heirs or creditors of your family's victims.
- Claim passage, berth or credit on the name alone.
- Tell another PC how your ancestor ended.
- Keep a family custom that costs you.
- Find a stranger wearing your family's name.
- Quiet a room by saying who you are.
- Acquire a bond who served your line, and expects.
- Sail a route your family charted, and pay its toll.

## MAJOR BEATS

- Take Major Reputation fallout.
- Assume a role or burden the line is known for.
- Face the living consequence of the famous deed.
- Be disowned or denounced by those who claimed your blood.
- Unearth the line's buried truth: a crime, a bargain, an heir.
- Make the choice your ancestor couldn't.
- End a piece of the legacy forever: the debt, the feud, the flag.
- Another PC suffers fallout from a feud your blood began.
- Reclaim a family right, and find it dearer to hold than to win.
- Begin something your own heirs will have to carry.

## ZENITH BEATS

- Become the one the line is remembered by, eclipsing all who came before.
- Pay the line's last debt, burn its flag, and sail out from under the name.

Roll 1d10 on the following table or choose to determine what you carry with you to mark your calling:

1. A signet that opens doors it shouldn't
2. Their portrait, with your face in it
3. The log of their last voyage, ending mid-sentence
4. An ancestral debt, still legally valid; the collectors persist
5. Their undelivered letter, memorised
6. A worthless object they treasured, given to you without explanation
7. Their weapon, repaired into technically a different weapon — except it isn't
8. A deed to something someone else currently holds
9. Their uncracked cipher; you've worked it for years
10. Their name, tattooed on you before you could refuse
