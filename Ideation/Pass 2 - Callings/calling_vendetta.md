# VENDETTA

*The obsession with a single target, and the ruin it brings*

Somebody took something from you that cannot be replaced. A ship, a crew, a family, your name. Every sail you run down is practice for the one that matters, and you will not stop until you have looked them in the eye. You have no idea what comes after. That is the problem.

> **CORE ABILITY — THE ACCOUNT.** Name your target at character creation: a person, an institution, or a fleet. Once per session, when you act directly to advance your vendetta, clear D4 stress from Blood.

**Dominant track:** Blood.

## Character Questions

- What did they take from you, and what will you do when it is over?
- Choose another player character. They once pulled you back from a chance at your target. Do you thank them or resent them?
- Choose another player character. They are the only one who knows the whole story. Why did you tell them?
- What mark of the wrong do you carry where others can see it?

## Minor Beats

- Take Minor Blood fallout pressing on when withdrawal was wiser.
- Encounter someone who knows your target personally.
- Damage something your target values: property, reputation, a loyal lieutenant.
- Refuse an offered peace, settlement, or forgiveness.
- Find evidence that your target is more complicated than you believed.
- Put another PC at risk because of the vendetta.
- Learn something new about what your target did.
- Meet a stranger who suffered at the same hands.
- Lie to an ally about what you are doing.
- Acquire a bond with intelligence on your target's movements.
- Claim or deface something of your target's as a message.
- Follow a lead into waters a tier deeper than the crew agreed to sail.
- Describe what was taken from you, in full, to another PC.
- Let hatred steer you past a better choice.
- Receive word that your target knows you are coming.
- Help someone with their own grievance to gain allies for yours.
- Spend a D8 or higher resource on a lead that goes cold.
- Show mercy your target would never show you.

## Major Beats

- Take Major Blood fallout in direct pursuit of your vendetta.
- Destroy a major asset of your target: a flagship, a stronghold, a trusted lieutenant.
- Be captured, cornered, or held by your target's people.
- Learn something that genuinely complicates your hatred.
- Cause another PC or one of your bonds lasting harm through your pursuit.
- Be offered a way to end it without confrontation, and choose.
- Discover that someone now hunts you the way you hunt your target.
- Your target moves against you first, and the crew pays for it.
- Ally with a power you despise — the Aspis, a rival Free Captain — against your common enemy.
- Achieve a real victory against your target that is not enough.

## Zenith Beats

- ***The Reckoning.*** Face your target, and choose what you do with the moment.
- ***The Inheritance.*** Pass the vendetta on — willingly or not — and live with who you are without it.
- ***The Downfall.*** The vendetta becomes you. Let it finish what it started, or burn it out and see who is left in the ash.

## What You Carry

*Roll 1d10 or choose:*

1. A piece of your target's property — taken, not given — carried as proof of intent.
2. A scar your target gave you, which you have refused to have properly treated.
3. A list of your target's allies and ports of call, updated in your own hand.
4. A loaded pistol kept for this purpose alone. You have named it. You tell no one the name.
5. A letter to your target, never sent, explaining everything.
6. Your target's ship's name, carved into driftwood, carried as a ward and a promise.
7. A court document proving the wrong, which no court will ever hear.
8. A portrait of your target so detailed you would know them in any disguise.
9. A small piece of what was taken from you — the only piece that survived.
10. Knots in a cord, counting how long you have been in pursuit.
