# PIRACY — The Shared Calling

*The beats of the life itself, held in common by every pirate*

This is the life — the ship, the sea, the prize, the port, the Code, and the slow accumulation of enemies and debts. Every character holds this calling alongside their personal one. At the end of each session, choose two active beats from either pool; there is no hierarchy between them.

Piracy carries the life so the personal callings can carry the obsession. The storms, the shares, and the Code live here; the thing that makes your character different from every other pirate lives in your personal calling.

## Minor Beats

### The Ship & the Sea

- Survive a storm, a rogue wave, or the weather off the Eye.
- Make a navigational call under pressure that the crew must live with.
- Run from a superior sail and make it — or turn and face it.
- Take Minor Blood fallout from what the sea does to bodies.
- Board a prize and deal with what you find there.
- Complete a journey through Tier 2 waters or deeper.
- Stand a night watch alone, and reach port changed.

### Ports & the Shore

- Take Minor Gold fallout from what ports do to shares.
- Acquire a bond in a port: a fence, a tavern-keeper, a harbour official.
- Be recognised in port for something you did — or didn't.
- Get into trouble ashore that has nothing to do with your obsession.
- Have your cargo inspected and held by a port authority.
- Drink a port dry on the crew's coin, and be welcome back anyway.

### The Code & the Politics

- Invoke the Code in a dispute, win or lose.
- Deal with another crew without an incident becoming an incident.
- Take Minor Reputation fallout from the Shackles noticing you.
- Be asked to do something the Code permits and your stomach doesn't.
- See the Free Captains' politics up close, and leave changed in your understanding.

### The Economics of Plunder

- Take a prize, and discover what it is actually worth.
- Divide shares, and have the division bite.
- Take Minor Luck fallout.
- Lose a prize to weather, pursuit, or a decision that seemed reasonable at the time.
- Sell something you stole to someone who knows where it came from.

## Major Beats

- Survive something that should have killed you, with the crew watching.
- Take Major Blood fallout, and keep sailing.
- Come to the formal attention of the Chelish Navy, the Aspis Consortium, or a rival Free Captain.
- Lose a ship, and pay everything that costs.
- Make an enemy of a captain with the standing to make it matter.
- Help change the political landscape of the Shackles: a Council vote, a port changing hands.
- Take Major Reputation fallout as the Shackles reassesses who you are.
- Gain or lose enough that the ship's quality, or a haunt you rely on, changes a dice size.
- Hear a story about your deed in a port you have never visited, told wrong.
- Choose between the Code and your own judgement, with lasting consequences.

## No Zenith Beats

Piracy has no zeniths, deliberately. The life does not end; people do. Endings belong to the personal callings — Piracy is the sea they happen on.
